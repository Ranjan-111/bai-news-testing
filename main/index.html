KEY HIGHLIGHTS OF PM MODI'S INDEPENDENCE DAY SPEECH (2025)

BIG PUSH ON TECHNOLOGY AND INDUSTRY:

Announced mission-mode focus on semiconductors with a goal to roll out India's first made-in-India chip, and a call to develop indigenous jet engines. Emphasis on self-reliance in critical tech as part of the 2047 developed-India vision.

GST REFORMS:

Signaled “second-generation” GST rationalisation to simplify slabs, reduce compliance burden (especially for MSMEs), and improve ease of doing business.

GOVERNANCE AND DE-REGULATION:

Said a task force will review and prune outdated rules and compliances to lower friction for businesses, with specific attention to logistics, exports, and startup ecosystems. Complex procedures for permits and licenses can delay projects, product launches, and shipments.

15 AUGUST 2025

ENERGY STRATEGY:

Set an ambition to expand nuclear power capacity roughly tenfold by 2047 to meet clean energy and growth needs.

JOBS AND YOUTH:

Proposed a ₹1,00,000 crore employment push aimed at skilling, apprenticeships, and catalyzing job creation through MSMEs and startups.

SOCIAL COMPACT:

Urged “Vocal for Local,” heritage pride, and unity as pillars for reaching a developed India by 2047, framing it as a national mission beyond party lines.

NATIONAL SECURITY POSTURE:

Delivered a firm message on terrorism deterrence and reiterated resolve on water and border-related issues, linking security to economic goals.

DEMOGRAPHY MISSION:

Announced a high-powered “Demography Mission” to harness India's youth bulge through education, health, and productivity-focused programs.
